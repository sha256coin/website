CIRCULATING SUPPLY CALCULATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To calculate circulating supply at any block height:

  1. Determine which halving period: period = block_height / 420,000
  2. Calculate full periods: full_periods_coins = 42,000,000 × (1 - (1/2)^period)
  3. Calculate partial period:
       blocks_in_current = block_height % 420,000
       reward = 100 / (2^period)
       partial_coins = blocks_in_current × reward
  4. Total = full_periods_coins + partial_coins


NOTES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- The actual supply will be slightly less than 84M due to rounding in the
  right-shift operation (nSubsidy >>= halvings) after many halvings
- Lost coins, burn addresses, and unclaimed rewards will further reduce
  the effective circulating supply
- After 64 halvings (~1024 years), block rewards become 0 due to the check
  in GetBlockSubsidy() that prevents undefined right-shift behavior
